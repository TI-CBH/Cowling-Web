About Cowling Electric

Established in 2011, Cowling Electric has built a strong reputation in commercial service, residential service, and emergency response. Available 24/7/365, we're committed to providing reliable electrical solutions whenever you need them.

Our comprehensive services include specialized solutions such as Thermal Image Scanning, EV Charging Stations, Energy Management Evaluations, and Emergency Lighting Systems Audits. We offer Surge Suppression Device installation for both individual electrical panels and whole building protection.

Through our Suite Evaluation Program, we assist property owners and managers in preparing buildings or lease spaces for new tenants, ensuring all electrical systems meet the highest standards of safety and efficiency.
